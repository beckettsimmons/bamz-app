In places nasturtiums can produce ground-covering masses but seem to do this mostly where the ground has been disturbed. They do not seem to establish themselves in areas of stable vegetation. The nasturtium was introduced for garden use and soon became naturalized in the wild. They flower throughout the year but more profusely in summer.
